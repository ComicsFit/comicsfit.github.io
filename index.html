Features

One job. Done well.

Compress, resize and optimize comic archives for your e-reader. Pick your device, pick your output format, hit Optimize.

  • Smart compression Dramatically smaller files while keeping the visual quality.
  • Device presets Kobo Libra Color, Kindle Scribe, reMarkable 2, or a custom resolution. ComicsFit handles the resizing.
  • Truly native Built with Swift on macOS and GTK4/Adwaita on Linux. Fast and light at ~20 MB.
  • Batch processing Queue up your whole collection and walk away.
  • Manga mode Right-to-left page ordering for KEPUB and AZW3 output, so your manga reads correctly on-device.
